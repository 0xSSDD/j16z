The Problem

Merger-arb desks waste hours every day hunting for deal updates across fragmented sources.

Labor Drag

1,250+ hours/year of low-leverage data work per desk, worth $150K+ in fully-loaded analyst cost

P&L Drag

Missed dockets and late regulatory news = 50-200 bps of avoidable slippage on affected positions

Regulatory Complexity

Multi-layered FTC/DOJ approval, extended outside dates, heightened antitrust scrutiny in 2026

Operational Risk

No unified audit trail of "what did we know, when?" and no single PM view across all active deals

Information scattered across EDGAR, CourtListener, FTC/DOJ sites, terminals, and spreadsheets
